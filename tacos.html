Taco Recipe

Description:

To make classic ground beef tacos, start by heating a tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add one pound of lean ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat, then stir in two teaspoons each of chili powder and cumin, half a teaspoon each of oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper, along with two tablespoons of tomato paste and half a cup of water. Let the mixture simmer for 4 minutes until the sauce thickens. While the meat simmers, warm 8 corn or flour tortillas in a skillet or microwave. To assemble the tacos, spoon the seasoned beef onto each tortilla and top with your favorite ingredients such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped red onions, shredded cheese, sour cream, guacamole, or salsa. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade tacos!

Ingredients:

  • beef
  • torillas
  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • onion

Steps to prepare:

  1. season and cook the beef
  2. heat up the torillas
  3. chop lettuce, tomato and onion
  4. plate the servings and enjoy
